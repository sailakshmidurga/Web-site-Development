Tirupati Temple
Andhra Pradesh
Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (a form of Vishnu), the temple is located atop the sacred Tirumala hills.
Meenakshi Temple
Madurai
The Meenakshi Amman Temple is a stunning Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva).
Ooty
Tamil Nadu
Ooty is a scenic hill station nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, often called the "Queen of Hill Stations".
Marina Beach
Chennai
Marina Beach is the longest natural urban beach in India, stretching along the Bay of Bengal.
